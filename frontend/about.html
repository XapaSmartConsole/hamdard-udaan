Objective

To drive secondary sales through Wholesalers (W2) by incentivizing performance and ensuring structured participation across the channel. The scheme aims to boost engagement, streamline tracking, and reward consistent performance.

Enrolment Guidelines

  • This scheme applies only to Hamdard products within the OTC Division.
  • Eligible criteria: Sub stockiest who will purchase the goods from mapped and authorised Super Distributor only.
  • All sub stockiest must be completed through the program and share all required documents like PAN, GST by the sales team. Required documents include the retailer's PAN card, outlet image, address proof, GST certificate of application, and bank details with a cancelled cheque.
  • POS booking: All user enrolling slabs must be completed the SFA (Sales Force Automation) platform only.
  • Retailer proof of purchase will be retrieved from the DBT software for the relevant period to verify their achievement in the enrolled slab.
  • At the end of each slab, achievements will be validated and locked by respective sales managers before the cut-off date.
  • Enrolments and approvals will be digitally documented and stored for audit purposes.
  • All secondary sales must be recorded in DBT to qualify for incentives.
  • SFA use DBT integration for seamless tracking and validation.
  • Achievement payouts to wholesalers will be directly disbursed through Direct Bank Transfer (DBT) by the promotion agency.
  • Management reserves the right to revise, modify, or withdraw the program without providing the reason, and the Channel Partners accepts these terms and conditions.
